In my freetime, I love meeting up with friends and doing sport, mostly swimming, bouldering, hiking as well as going to the gym.
I mostly aim to spend quality time with those nearest to me. 
After all, its all about enjoying the important experiences rather than chasing illusive ideals or material goods.


### Volunteering at [Johanniter Notuebernachtung Ohlauer Strasse](https://www.johanniter.de/juh/lv-bb/rv-berlin/unsere-standorte-und-einrichtungen-in-berlin/einrichtungs-detailseite/wohnungslosenhilfe-berlin-4533/)
(January 2025 - Ongoing, ~5hrs / Week)

Furthermore, I volunteer at a local homeless shelter organized by the Johanniter e.V. where we provide guests with a 
warm meal for the evening, offer them a bed to come back to as well as securely storing their belongings for the night. 
Futhermore, we hand out clothes and provide sanitary facilities as well as the option to consult a doctor or social worker. 

I think its important to contribute to ones' community and I feel its a very important issue that needs more attention and care even though its something most people see on a daily basis.

A not so fun-fact: did you know that in the entirety of berlin, there are only about 200 beds in **total** for homeless men (in non-winter months) :(?
